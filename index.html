QUESTIONS YOU HAVE.

When will I get it?

Pre-order runs until June 25, 2026. Then we start shipping — within the EU usually 5–10 business days.

How explicit is it?

A full spectrum — from gentle illustrations (a couple in a sauna, a nude in the woods) to explicit (BDSM, glory hole, water sports). To be direct: if you're looking for a mandala book, this isn't for you.

Who drew it?

A team of queer artists who wish to remain anonymous. Adult queer work in a portfolio can complicate other commissions for them. We respect that. Their work speaks for itself.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, across the entire EU. For the UK and US we ship as an international parcel. Shipping cost is calculated at checkout.
